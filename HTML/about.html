About Us

Our Story:

Discover the enchanting journey behind Home Appeal. Born from a love of design, our founder, Aakeefah Jones, turned a passion for creating beautiful spaces into a thriving home decor haven. Explore our curated collection at Home Appeal and bring a touch of personalized elegance to your home. Crafting stories, one piece at a time.

Our Mission:

The purpose of Home Appeal is to inspire and enhance the beauty of homes by offering thoughtfully crafted and personalized home decor pieces. We believe that every home has a unique story to tell, and our purpose is to provide individuals with the means to express their style. With a commitment to quality craftsmanship, authenticity, and customer satisfaction, Home Appeal aims to create a warm and inviting atmosphere in every home, turning spaces into personal sanctuaries that reflect the individuality and stories of those who inhabit them.

Our Team:

At Home Appeal, our team is a tight-knit family united by a passion for creativity and a love for crafting beautiful stories within homes. We foster a culture of collaboration, innovation, and attention to detail. Each member brings a unique skill set, contributing to the vibrant tapestry of our company culture. Together, we are dedicated to delivering personalized, high-quality home decor that reflects the heart and soul of Home Appeal.
